Costa Rica: A Nature and Cultural Immersion

As a computer science major, I wanted a way to provide a diverse perspective to my current skills and thinking. To accomplish this, I decided to study in Costa Rica for winter break of 2019 as a way to immerse into a vibrant culture that was completely different than what I was familiar with.

This project worked to capture the culture experienced on home stays in working family farms, the ecosystems of the tropics hiking between farms, and eco-tourism in Costa Rica. Through a mapping system and immersive 360 photo spheres, this project documents our geographical class explorations and virtual reality immersive experiences.
